# How much does a taxi cost in Cuba in 2025?

> Updated and authoritative guide to taxi prices in Cuba, including airport transfers and intercity routes from Havana (HAV) and Varadero (VRA). Prices are fixed per car, not per person.

## Key Facts
- Taxi prices in Cuba are typically quoted **per vehicle**, not per passenger.
- Rates vary by **route, distance, vehicle type**, and **time of service**.
- CubanTaxis offers **private transfers** with fixed prices and no hidden fees.
- Airport pick-ups include **flight monitoring**.

## What Affects Taxi Prices in Cuba?

The final taxi price may depend on:
- Distance and travel time between cities
- Vehicle type (classic/modern car vs. minivan)
- Daytime vs. nighttime service
- Airport waiting time and parking
- Optional sightseeing stops
- Pick-up location outside city centers

---

## Practical Tips for Tourists

- Booking in advance helps **lock a fixed fare**.
- Sharing your **flight number** is recommended for airport transfers.
- Private taxis are generally the **most convenient and reliable option**.
- Payment is usually made **on arrival** in USD, EUR, or local currency.
