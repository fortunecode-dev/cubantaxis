# How much is a taxi in Cuba, how to get one, and other questions

> Official answers to common questions about taxi services, prices, payments, vehicles, and transfers in Cuba provided by CubanTaxis.

## General Taxi Questions in Cuba

### Can I pay for the taxi in dollars, euros, or local currency (CUP)?
Yes. Payments are accepted in **USD, EUR, and Cuban pesos (CUP)**. Customers can choose the most convenient currency when confirming their transfer.

### What happens if my flight is delayed?
CubanTaxis monitors flight arrivals. If a flight is delayed, the driver will wait **at no extra cost**, provided the correct flight number was supplied during booking.

### Do you offer private excursions in addition to transfers?
Yes. CubanTaxis offers **private excursions and tours** to destinations such as **Viñales, Cienfuegos, and Trinidad**, in addition to standard transfers.

### What types of vehicles do you offer for transfers?
Available vehicle options include:
- Classic cars
- Convertibles
- Modern vehicles
- Minivans

Vehicle availability depends on passenger count and customer preference.

### Can I request a night transfer?
Yes. CubanTaxis operates **24/7**. Night transfers may include a **small additional surcharge**.

### How do I know if my transfer is confirmed?
Once confirmed, customers receive a **direct confirmation message** via **WhatsApp or email**, including trip details and the driver’s name.
